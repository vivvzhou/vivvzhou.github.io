Breaking Free

Medium:

Mixed media with gouache and pencil

Size:

14 x 12 in.

Description:

In this piece, I come to terms with my reserved nature by recognizing the beauty held within. Though I'm naturally reserved around others, my shell can be broken with time and memories, revealing my inner qualities and character as a person not seen by those who just take a glance at me. Every person is deep with thoughts and emotion that require time to understand and relate to. I emphasize this by using a variety of colors to contrast the inner beauty to the broken outside shell. I draw the "shell" to resemble that of paper or skin to portray the fragileness of one's identity. It's like they say, "don't judge a book by its cover" because anyone's outer shell may not be representative of who they really are on the inside.
